Download

To verify with certainty the identity of the services offered by Freepto , you must download and install our CA on your computer.

wget http://ca.freepto.mx/ca.pem

Verify

It's strongly recommended to verify the authenticity of the certificate downloaded . You can do it in a few simple steps:

  • Download our GPG key:

    • gpg --search-key 364B68D4

  • Check the key fingerprint:

    • This step is necessary to ensure that the gpg key downloaded is really our

    Check that the fingerprint shown by the following command,

    gpg --fingerprint 364B68D4

    is equal to that shown below:

    Key fingerprint = C355 97C8 7BE0 8D11 A5D8 4087 909C 6C75 364B 68D4

  • Check the certificate:

    • Download the signature of the certificate:

    wget http://ca.freepto.mx/ca.pem.sig

    At this point verify the certificate using gpg:

    $ gpg --verify ca.pem.sig ca.pem
    gpg: Signature made Mon 03 Nov 2014 23:45:25 GMT using RSA key ID 364B68D4
    gpg: Good signature from "Freepto Repository "

Instal

To install the certificate on your computer, follow these steps:

$ sudo -s
# apt-get install ca-certificates
# wget -O /usr/local/share/ca-certificates/freepto.pem http://ca.freepto.mx/ca.pem
# update-ca-certificates
